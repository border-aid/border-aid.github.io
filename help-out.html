Buy Supplies

Unless you're purchasing an item from our Amazon wishlist, you'll need to coordinate with a member of the border aid community to deliver the supplies to the right location.
  • Amazon wishlist for Jacumba sites
  • Purchase blankets, jackets, sweatshirts, or sweaters from a bulk secondhand store - we suggest the Goodwill warehouses in San Ysidro or Escondido

Volunteer

  • Sign up to participate in Jacumba support
  • Sign up for an airport support shift
  • Sign up to be an on-call translator
  • Sign up as a general on-the-ground volunteer with Detention Resistance
  • Sign up to participate in street release support

Caution

Do not publicly share exact locations of aid sites or photos with unblurred faces. This may put migrants and volunteers in danger of harrassment or violence.
