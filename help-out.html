  • Sign up to participate in Jacumba support

  • Sign up to be an on-call translator
  • Sign up as a general on-the-ground volunteer with Detention Resistance
  • Sign up to participate in street release support

Caution

Do not publicly share exact locations of aid sites or photos with unblurred faces. This may put migrants and volunteers in danger of harrassment or violence.

We need journalists who value the lives of migrants to report on the reality at the border - not just regurgitate whatever CBP says is the truth. Please contact some of the organizations holding it down on the ground for comments!
  • American Friends Service Committee - contact Pedro Rios, Adriana Jasso, or Benjamin Prado at usmexborder@afsc.org or call 619-233-4114
