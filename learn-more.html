Page Purpose

This page collects additional media on borders, migration, and enforcement, providing a broader perspective on the patterns of history that the phenomenon of open-air detention sites is part of. For an introduction to open-air detention sites and the role of mutual aid at the border, see the What's Happening? page.

Web Pages and Datasets

Map of Border Surveillance Towers

A map and dataset created by the Electronic Frontier Foundation documenting the location and type of various surveillance technologies deployed by CBP in the US' southern borderlands. Read more about the dataset in this blog post from EFF.

Border Militarization Research Hub

A data/research hub from the Southern Border Communities Coalition that provides a deep look at border militarization, the border agents masquerading as soldiers, the violent and deadly border wall, and the policies that allow this to happen.

Arizona OpenGIS Initiative for Deceased Migrants

A searchable map of migrant deaths in Arizona, made from data shared by the Pima County Office of the Medical Examiner with Humane Borders
