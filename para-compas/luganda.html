Bw’oba oli mu nkambi y’ensalo *

Buuka ku bikulu ebirimu

Ebimu ku bikulu nga gavumenti yaffe ekusibira ebweru nga tekuwadde mmere, mazzi, obujjanjabi, oba aw’okusula:

  • Bw’olaba ensonga y’obujjanjabi ey’amangu, nsaba otegeeze bannakyewa oba Border Patrol mu bwangu.
  • Osangibwa mu emu ku nkambi zino:
    • Ekiwonvu ky’Omwezi – Enkambi ya O’Neil MuKoPah Okufuluma 77, Jacumba CA
    • Willows – 43475 Oluguudo olukadde 80, Jacumba CA
    • Enkambi 177 – Oluguudo lw’ettaka ku nkomerero ya Jewel Valley Rd, Boulevard CA
  • NSABA ETTAKKA MUSABA EKISEERA . Nsaba mulonde kasasiro era mwewale okwokya ebimera.
  • Tetumanyi bbanga ki ly’onoomala wano. Kiyinza okuba essaawa oba ennaku.
  • Bannakyewa bagenda kuba bawa amazzi g'emmere n'obujjanjabi obusookerwako emirundi ebiri olunaku .
  • Bw’onoova wano, kirabika ojja kutwalibwa mu kifo ekirongoosa ebintu mu kitundu kyo:
    • Tetumanyi bbanga ki lye kinaatwala okukulongoosa okuva mu kifo ekyo.
    • Kakasa nti oyambala ekintu ekibuguma olw’okusibirwa okwo.
    • Kakasa nti oggalawo essimu yo nga tonnalinnya bbaasi okuva mu nkambi.
    • Bw’oba otambula n’ab’omu maka go, olina okutegeeza Border Patrol ani gw’otambula naye okukendeeza ku mikisa gy’okwawukana . Tolina kwawukana na munno oba abaana bo. Fuba okusigala nga muli wamu okutuusa nga muyimbuddwa okuva mu kaduukulu. Ebisingawo ku buyambi eri ab‟omu maka abaawukanye osobola okubisanga wansi.
    • Okuva mu kifo ekirongoosa abantu mu kitundu, ojja kuyimbulwa oba okusindikibwa mu kkomera ly’abayingira mu ggwanga, oba okulinnyisibwa ennyonyi okugenda e Texas okulongoosebwa. Abantu ssekinnoomu abasinga bagenda kuyimbulwa mu bifo ebiri mu San Diego oba Riverside Counties.
      • SAN DIEGO EYIMUDDWA :
        • Bw’oba otambula n’abaana abato, ng’olina ebyetaago by’obujjanjabi, olina obulemu mu mubiri, mu magezi, oba mu nkulaakulana, ng’oli lubuto, ng’olina emyaka egisukka mu 55, oba LGBTQ+, ojja kuweebwa eky’okusalawo okugenda mu kifo we basula. Ojja kusobola okubeera mu kifo kino okumala ennaku 1-2 era ojja kufuna obuyambi mu mateeka, mu by’obujjanjabi, n’okugenda mu maaso.
        • Bw’oba oli muntu mukulu ali obwannamunigina oba ng’oli mu kibinja ky’abantu abakulu bonna, Border Patrol ejja kukusuula ku siteegi y’okuyita. Endagiriro eno eri 3120 Iris Ave. San Ysidro, CA 92173.
          • Bw’oba osula mu kitundu ky’e San Diego oli wa ddembe okukuba essimu ya siponsa wo n’okutegeka entambula okutuuka mu maka go amapya.
          • Bw’oba otambula ebweru w’ekitundu kya San Diego, ekyuma ekikutambuza ku bwereere kijja kukutwala mu Old Town Transit Center, okuva gy’osobola okusanga eky’obwereere okutuuka ku kisaawe ky’ennyonyi n’okutuuka ku siteegi z’eggaali y’omukka ne bbaasi. Ojja kusuubirwa okusasula wooteeri yo, emmere n’entambula. Wifi ya bwereere ku kisaawe era osobola okugulayo SIM card bwe kiba kyetaagisa.
      • RIVERSIDE COUNTY EBIFULUMIDDWA:
        • Oyinza okusuulibwa mu kifo ekiddukanyizibwa eggye ly’obulokozi. Ojja kusobola okumala ennaku 1-2 era ojja kufuna obuyambi mu mateeka, mu by’obujjanjabi, n’okugenda mu maaso. Singa famire yo yonna yasuulibwa mu San Diego, Salvation Army esobola okukwatagana n’ebitongole ebitali bya magoba mu San Diego okukugatta.
      • BWOBA OSIDIKA MU KIFO EKIKWATA KU ABANTU
        • Bw’oba olina ebyafaayo by’okuyingira mu nsi oba eby’obumenyi bw’amateeka, otera okusindikibwa mu kifo ekikuumirwamu abayingira mu ggwanga. Oyinza okusindikibwa mu kifo ekiri mu California oba mu ssaza eddala.
        • Bw’oba olina omuntu gw’okwatagana naye mu Amerika, alina okukozesa Immigration Legal Directory eyungiddwa wansi okukufunira munnamateeka okumpi ne w’osibirwa
        • Ojja kuba olina okuyita mu “Credible Fear Interview” okusobola okuyimbulwa okuva mu kaduukulu. Mu mboozi eno, ojja kunnyonnyola lwaki otya okudda mu nsi yo. Bw’otoyita mu interview eno, oyinza okugobwa mu nsi
        • Bw’oyita mu Credible Fear Interview yo, ojja kwetaaga siponsa alina embeera y’amateeka mu Amerika okuwandiika ebbaluwa eraga nti osobola okubeera nabo ng’osaba obubudamu. Olwo, Omukungu wo ow’okugoba abantu ajja kusalawo oba osobola okuyimbulwa. Singa oyimbulwa, ojja kufuna olunaku lwa kkooti okumpi ne siponsa wo gy’abeera. Bw’otoyimbulwa, ojja kuba olina okusaba obubudamu mu kifo we basibira.
      • BW’OBA OLUTWIDDWA NNYONYU OKUGENDA MU TEXAS OBA OSIDIKA MU ARIZONA OKUKOLA:
        • Olw’omuwendo omunene ogw’ababundabunda abasala ensalo ya California, Border Patrol eyinza okukubuuka ku nnyonyi/bbaasi okugenda e Texas oba Arizona okulongoosebwa. Okuva awo, oyinza okuyimbulwa mu kitundu oba okusindikibwa mu kifo ekikuumirwamu abantu aba ICE.
  • Bw’oba oyawukanye ku maka go nga okola ku nsonga eno: Bw’oba oyimbuddwa okugenda e San Diego, noonya ebibiina “Al Otro Lado” ne “Immigrant Defenders,” ebiyinza okukuyamba okuddamu okwegatta n’ab’omu maka go. Bw’oba oyimbulwa mu kifo ekikuumirwamu abantu mu San Diego oba Riverside, abakozi b’ekifo kino basobola okukwatagana n’ebitongole ebitali bya magoba mu mateeka okuyamba okukugatta. Bw’oba osindikiddwa ku nnyonyi/bbaasi okugenda mu kifo ekirala, oba nga tewali bakozi batali ba magoba abaliwo ku mukutu gwa San Diego ogufulumya, oyinza okukuba oba okuweereza obubaka bwa WhatsApp ku 323-542-4582 okuyambibwa.
  • Bw’oba osindikiddwa mu ddwaaliro nga tebannaba kulongoosebwa: Bw’osiibulwa mu ddwaaliro, osobola okukuba ku +1 (619) 800-2083 (tesangibwa ku WhatsApp). Ye nnamba y’essimu eddukanyizibwa bannakyewa oluusi esobola okufuna eby’obugagga okusinziira ku byetaago byo. Bw’oba olina paasipooti, oli wa ddembe okugenda gy’osembayo. Wi-Fi ya bwereere ku kisaawe kino. Era tekiba kya bulabe okusula ku kisaawe ky’ennyonyi bw’oba tolina busobozi bwa kisenge kya wooteeri ng’olinda ennyonyi yo. Bw’omala okutuuka gy’ogenda, olina okunoonya munnamateeka n’owaayo okusaba kw’obubudamu amangu ddala nga bwe kisoboka, naye nga tebunnawera mwaka gumu oluvannyuma lw’olunaku lw’oyingira mu Amerika.
  • Bwoba osaba Asylum, olina okunoonya munnamateeka . Bino bye bimu ku bikozesebwa okuyamba okuzuula omuntu akiikirira mu mateeka:
    • https://www.abalwanirizi b’ensonga z’abayingira mu ggwanga.org/legaldirectory/
    • https://www.americanbar.org/groups/public_interest/okuyingira mu nsi/pulojekiti_enteekateeka/pulojekiti -obwenkanya-okuyingira mu nsi/tutuukirira/ oba kuba +1 (619) 255-8810
  • Bwe kiba kisoboka, wandiika by’oyitamu mu nkambi eno n’ebyo by’oyitamu okutuusa ng’oyimbuddwa okuva mu kaduukulu.
  • Ba agenti ba Border Patrol tebatera kuba ba mpisa. Oluusi balimba oba bakozesa abantu. Kozesa okwegendereza ng’okolagana nabo.
Okusobola okwanguyirwa okukozesa ababundabunda be tuweereza, omukutu guno guyinza okuyita ebifo eby’okusibiramu abantu mu bbanga nga ‘enkambi z’ensalo’, ‘ebifo eby’ebweru w’okusibira abantu’, oba ebifaananako bwe bityo. Kyokka tokola nsobi: bino byonna bikyali bifo bye bimu eby’okusibirwamu abantu nga bikuumibwa CBP.
