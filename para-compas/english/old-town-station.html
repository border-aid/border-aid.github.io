Airport Shuttle

There is a free airport shuttle from Old Town Transit Center. Itâ€™s called the San Diego Flyer and it picks up and drops off passengers at two locations:

  • On Pacific Highway at the west side of the Transit Center
  • On the east side of the transit center near the depot building (out on the island - see map below)
The San Diego Flyer operates approximately every 20-30 minutes, seven days a week. Pick-up and drop-offs are timed to meet the first and last Trolley, Coasters, Amtrak trains, and MTS busses with the first pick-up at 4:45 a.m. and the last pick-up/drop-off at 12:30 a.m.
