Tips When Soliciting Volunteer Translators

  • In addition to asking for a phone number and any language proficiencies, be sure to ask translation volunteers which hours of the day and days of the week are appropriate for phone calls
  • Reach out to local cultural clubs, at universities or otherwise, for help in finding volunteers for specific languages
